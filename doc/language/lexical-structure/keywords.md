# Keywords

```ebnf
keyword ::= normal-keyword |
            type-keyword |
            reserved-keyword
```

```ebnf
normal-keyword ::= 'and' |
                   'as' |
                   'assert' |
                   'break' |
                   'catch' |
                   'cond' |
                   'const' |
                   'defer' |
                   'else' |
                   'err' |
                   'ext' |
                   'false' |
                   'fn' |
                   'for' |
                   'if' |
                   'in' |
                   'let' |
                   'match' |
                   'mod' |
                   'mut' |
                   'next' |
                   'nil' |
                   'not' |
                   'opaque' |
                   'or' |
                   'pub' |
                   'raise' |
                   'rec' |
                   'recv' |
                   'ret' |
                   'tail' |
                   'test' |
                   'true' |
                   'try' |
                   'type' |
                   'use' |
                   'while' |
                   'with'
```

```ebnf
type-keyword ::= 'agent' |
                 'any' |
                 'atom' |
                 'bool' |
                 'handle' |
                 'int' |
                 'none' |
                 'real' |
                 'ref' |
                 'str'
```

```ebnf
reserved-keyword ::= 'friend' |
                     'macro' |
                     'meta' |
                     'quote' |
                     'unquote' |
                     'yield'
```
